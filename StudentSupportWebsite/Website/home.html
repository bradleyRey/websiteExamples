Welcome

Welcome to the Coventry University Support Centre. This site is designed to provide you with resources relating to maths, statistics and programming. Here you will find a Theta timetable along with information about Sigma and the Programming Support Centre. Additionally, you can search for and download programming worksheets, gain access to vital tutorial videos and book meeting slots with your tutors and lecturers using our booking

Our sectors


Theta


For Engineering and Computing students, the Theta Centre offers general support and guidance from faculty and course staff teaching on your modules. Please refer to the Theta timetable to select the more appropriate tutor to meet. Don’t just send an email, drop by either individually or as a small group to get the answers to your coursework and exam related questions.

Sigma


At the Sigma Centre, we provide students with mathematics and statistics support. Delivered in a relaxed and friendly environment by experienced tutors you will be able to get answers to your basic and complex questions. Whether you’re looking to solve simple or more advanced problems, our tutors will be able to support and guide you through the process of understanding and retaining the information in order to succeed. Please feel free to drop by and one of our tutors will be happy to help as soon as they’re available, no problem is too complex! Please find us on the ground floor of the Lanchester Library.

Programming


The Programming Support Centre runs a series of sessions for students in the Engineering and Computing Faculty which focusses on additional support regarding programming. Python, C++, Linux are the general topics for discussion, but if you need help with other languages, please make a request and the programming support team will accommodate appropriately. Find us in EC1-01 and EC1-29 every Wednesday. As this is a drop-in session, no bookings are required!
